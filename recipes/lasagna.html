Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that features layers of flat pasta noodles, rich meat or vegetable sauce, and creamy béchamel sauce. The layers are typically stacked together and baked to perfection. The dish is known for its comforting and hearty flavor. It's a favorite at family gatherings and restaurants alike. Lasagna can be customized with various fillings, including ground meat, spinach, mushrooms, and cheese. It's a beloved comfort food that's enjoyed by many around the world.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Meat or vegetable sauce
  • Béchamel sauce
  • Cheese (such as mozzarella and Parmesan)
  • Optional fillings (e.g., spinach, mushrooms)
  1. Preheat the oven to the desired temperature.
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  3. In a baking dish, layer the cooked noodles, meat or vegetable sauce, béchamel sauce, cheese, and optional fillings.
  4. Repeat the layering process until the dish is filled, finishing with a layer of sauce and cheese on top.
  5. Bake the lasagna in the preheated oven until the top is golden and bubbly.
  6. Remove the lasagna from the oven and let it cool slightly before serving.
